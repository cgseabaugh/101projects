Unbreakable Patches



Little to nothing is known about Patches and his background. He has been previously acquainted with Shiva of the East, Solaire of Astora, Lautrec of Carim, and Petrus of Thorolund. Patches' very existence implies that he is trans-universal, or at the very least has a version of himself in every reality; The Hyena in Demon Souls, the Spider in Bloodborne, and Trusty/Unbreakable in Dark Souls.
